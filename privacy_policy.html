Parlox Privacy Policy

Introduction

Parlox is committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

What Data Do We Collect?

We collect the following data from our users:

  • Username and password
  • Email address
  • Profile information (e.g. name, location, bio)

How Do We Use Your Data?

We use your data to:

  • Provide you with access to our services
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Communicate with you about our services and updates

How Do We Protect Your Data?

We take the following measures to protect your data:

  • We use encryption to protect your data in transit
  • We use secure servers to store your data
  • We limit access to your data to authorized personnel
